About the Foundation

Empowering academically gifted students through scholarships, bursaries, and educational support in the United Kingdom and Nigeria.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To honour the legacy of Dr. James Kenechukwu Onwubalili by providing educational scholarships, bursaries, and grants to academically gifted but financially disadvantaged students in the United Kingdom and Nigeria, enabling them to achieve their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

A world where every academically talented student, regardless of their financial background, has the opportunity to access quality education and fulfil their potential. We envision communities transformed by the ripple effects of empowered, educated individuals who go on to make significant contributions to their fields and societies.

What We Do

Our Objectives

The Foundation pursues its mission through clearly defined objectives that guide our scholarship programmes and educational initiatives.

  • To provide scholarships, bursaries, and educational grants to academically gifted students who lack financial means
  • To support students at secondary and tertiary levels of education in the United Kingdom and Nigeria
  • To partner with schools and educational institutions to identify and nurture exceptional talent
  • To offer maintenance grants and living allowances to support students throughout their studies
  • To celebrate and perpetuate the legacy of Dr. James Kenechukwu Onwubalili through educational empowerment
Our Support

How We Help

Scholarships

Full and partial scholarships covering tuition fees for outstanding students at secondary and tertiary institutions in the UK and Nigeria.

Bursaries

Financial awards to help students meet the costs of books, equipment, and other essential educational resources required for their studies.

Educational Grants

Targeted grants supporting specific educational needs, from examination fees to specialist training and professional development opportunities.

Maintenance Support

Living allowances and maintenance grants to ensure that students can focus on their studies without the burden of financial hardship.

Where We Operate

Geographic Focus

United Kingdom

JKOF (UK) is registered as a Charitable Incorporated Organisation in England and Wales. We work with partner schools and institutions across the UK to identify and support exceptional students from disadvantaged backgrounds, providing scholarships, bursaries, and maintenance grants.

Nigeria

JKOF (NIG), registration number 7061941, operates in Nigeria with a focus on supporting students in secondary and tertiary education. We partner with schools to identify academically gifted students who require financial assistance to continue their education and achieve their potential.
