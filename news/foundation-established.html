James Kenechukwu Onwubalili Foundation Established

Honouring a legacy of excellence through education

← Back to News

In July 2023, the family of Dr. James Kenechukwu Onwubalili formally established the James Kenechukwu Onwubalili Foundation (JKOF) to honour his extraordinary legacy and continue the work he believed in most deeply — empowering gifted students through education.

Dr. Onwubalili, a distinguished physician, nephrologist, and Fellow of the Royal College of Physicians, dedicated his life to medicine and education. His own journey — from a village school in Ogidi, Nigeria, to the highest echelons of British medicine — was made possible by the generosity of philanthropists who recognised his potential.

Without the support of philanthropists, JK may never have overcome his financial limitations. The Foundation continues his legacy by supporting intelligent, motivated students who face similar challenges.

Dual Operations

The Foundation operates as dual entities in both the United Kingdom and Nigeria, reflecting Dr. Onwubalili’s deep connections to both countries. The UK entity is registered as a Charitable Incorporated Organisation in England and Wales, whilst the Nigerian entity operates from Lagos with registration number 7061941.

Led by Dr. (Mrs.) Vivian Onwubalili as Chairperson, the Foundation’s Board of Trustees brings together distinguished professionals from medicine, academia, business, and public service — all united by their connection to Dr. JK and their commitment to his vision.

Our Mission

The Foundation’s mission is clear: to inspire, empower, and transform lives through educational opportunities. Through partnerships with schools and institutions across both countries, JKOF provides scholarships, bursaries, and grants to academically gifted but financially disadvantaged students.

Since its establishment, the Foundation has already awarded over 20 scholarships, working with seven partner schools to identify and support the next generation of leaders, physicians, and scholars.

Looking Ahead

As the Foundation grows, it remains guided by the values that defined Dr. JK’s life — excellence, service, and an unwavering belief in the transformative power of education. Every student supported by the Foundation carries forward his legacy, proving that with the right opportunity, talent can flourish regardless of circumstance.
