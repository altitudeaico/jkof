His Story

A Life Defined by Excellence

Dr. James Kenechukwu Onwubalili was a distinguished medical professional whose life embodied the transformative power of education. Born in Nigeria, he rose through academic excellence to become a Fellow of the Royal College of Physicians and a respected consultant in the United Kingdom.

Throughout his career, Dr. JK demonstrated an unwavering commitment to the highest standards of medical practice, earning numerous accolades including the Gold Medal from the Royal College of Physicians of Ireland.

His belief that every gifted student deserves the opportunity to fulfil their potential — regardless of financial circumstance — became the founding principle of the JKOF.
