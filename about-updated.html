About the Foundation

Empowering academically gifted students through scholarships, bursaries,
and educational support in the United Kingdom.

Our Mission

To inspire, empower and transform lives through educational opportunities

The Foundation honours the legacy of Dr. James Kenechukwu Onwubalili by providing educational scholarships, bursaries, and grants to academically gifted but financially disadvantaged students in the United Kingdom, enabling them to achieve their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

A world where talent flourishes regardless of circumstance

We envision a world where every academically talented student, regardless of their financial background, has the opportunity to access quality education and fulfil their potential. Communities transformed by the ripple effects of empowered, educated individuals who go on to make significant contributions to their fields and societies.

How We Help

Supporting Students at Every Stage

01

Scholarships

Full and partial scholarships covering tuition fees for outstanding students at secondary and tertiary institutions.

02

Bursaries

Financial awards to help students meet the costs of books, equipment, and essential educational resources.

03

Educational Grants

Targeted grants for examination fees, specialist training, and professional development opportunities.

04

Maintenance Support

Living allowances ensuring students can focus on their studies without the burden of financial hardship.

Where We Operate

One Mission, Two Countries

United Kingdom

Parent Foundation

JKOF (UK) is registered as a Charitable Incorporated Organisation in England and Wales (2026). As the parent foundation, we work with partner schools and institutions across the UK to identify and support exceptional students from disadvantaged backgrounds, providing scholarships, bursaries, and maintenance grants.

Nigeria

Affiliate Foundation

JKOF (NIG), registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (Registration: 7061941), is an affiliate of the parent UK foundation. It operates across Southern Nigeria, partnering with schools to identify academically gifted students who require financial assistance to continue their education.
