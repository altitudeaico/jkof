Why Give

Your Donation Changes Lives

Dr. James Kenechukwu Onwubalili dedicated his life to the pursuit of excellence and the belief that education is the most powerful instrument for change. The Foundation continues his legacy by providing scholarships, bursaries, and grants to academically gifted but financially disadvantaged students in the United Kingdom and Nigeria.

Every donation, no matter the size, directly supports a student's educational journey. Your contribution helps cover tuition fees, educational resources, and living costs — removing the financial barriers that stand between talented students and their potential.
